Panther

He is a great member of the Big Cat family, native to Asia, Africa and America. The panther is an incredibly intelligent and agile animal that is very rarely seen by humans in the wild, as they are usually very quiet and cautious animals. The panther is a carnivorous animal and is one of the most feared and powerful predators throughout its natural environment. Panther cubs are born blind and do not open their eyes until they are almost two weeks old.
